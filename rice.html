RICE & BEANS RECIPE

Description

Waakye is a dish of Ghanian style rice and beans. White rice and beans like black eyed peas, cow peas or red beans are boiled along with waakye leaves and a little salt until they are soft. There are no other flavorings or spices added. The dish is then paired with other foods to make a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • Dried black eyed peas, soaked overnight – 1 cup
  • White rice- 2 cups
  • Dried millet stalk leaves – 10
  • Water for cooking
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with about 4 cups of water.
  1. Add the millet leaf stalks
  1. Boil the water and millet leaf over high heat. The millet leaves will begin to turn the water a deep red color.
  1. Rinse the soaked beans and add the boiling water.
  1. Cover the beans let it cook until it is tender. Add water by the cup if the beans dry out before they are soft.
  1. Rinse the rice until the water is clear and add to the softbeans.
