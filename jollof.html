JOLLOF RICE RECIPE

Description

Jollof or jollof rice, is a rice dish from West Africa. The dish is typically made with long-grain rice, tomatoes, onions, spices, vegetables and meat

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red bell pepper / tatashe
  • 100ml vegetable oil
  • Maggi
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Put your rice into a bowl and soak with hot water.
